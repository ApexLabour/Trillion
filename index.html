More about Trillion Cleaning Services

Our goal is to enhance the appearance and ambiance of our clients’ homes and workplaces. Our cleaning services are provided on a contractual or once-off basis and we offer cleaning services for homes, corporate buildings and institutions.

As part of our service, we will:

  • Place trained, experienced staff on site.
  • Maintain cleaning consumables and PPE.
  • Provide staff with uniforms, PPE and specialised tools and equipment.
  • Conduct weekly site inspections and attend meetings as and when requested.
  • Tailor a cleaning plan according to your needs and create a cleaning schedule accordingly.

Our services are executed in accordance with a detailed schedule that is encapsulated in a detailed Service Level Agreement that specifies the frequency and scope of your chosen services.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question?

We've got you covered.

Check if any of these topics answer your question, if not just send us a message below and one of our friendly consultants will get in touch with you shortly to answer all of your questions.

1. Do I need to provide my own cleaning equipment and supplies?

No. You are not required to provide any supplies, cleaning products, a vacuum, carpet cleaner, or anything else. Each of our cleaners are outfitted with professional grade tools and cleaning supplies. If, however, you would rather we use your own supplies and/or products, just let us know! We can take customized requests if you would like a certain product used in your home or workplace.

2. Do I need to sign a lengthy contract?

No. You are welcome to use our services on a once-off basis, if preffered, however we do offer contracts for longer term solutions as required, please speak to one of our consultants for more information.

3. Is Trillion Cleaning Services insured?

Thank you for asking! It's very important to ask this question of any service provider or company that may be doing work in and around your home or workplace. To answer your question: yes, we are fully insured for your peace of mind and ours.

4. What if I need to cancel my appointment?

We do have a cancellation policy in place. We would require 3 full business days notice if you need to cancel or change any upcoming appointments. When you provide 3 or more full business days notice, there is no cancellation fee. If you provide less than 3 full business days notice, you will be charged 50% of your appointment cost to cover our preparation and transport costs. If you cancel your appointment on the day, or we cannot gain access into your home or workplace, you will be charged 100% of your appointment cost.

5. Is there anything Trillion Cleaning Services can't do?

There are some restrictions regarding weights and heights, pet messes, bodily fluids/waste, mold, and bugs, etc that need to be adhered to for health and safety concerns. Please speak to one of our friendly consultants for a full list.
